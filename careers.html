OPPORTUNITIES

American Inshore Divers Corp. is an Equal Opportunity Employer and a Drug Free Workplace. Our firm is always accepting resumes for Tenders, Diver-tenders, Divers and Diving Supervisors. We have great opportunities for those self-starting individuals who are looking for a career and are willing to grow with our company. It is very helpful to have a construction or construction related backround. We are also interested in self-motivated inside sales representatives who are experienced in the commercial diving industry and can successfully sell a wide range of commercial diving services. The ideal candidate will have a least 5 years inshore diving experience and 2 years sales experience. REQUIREMENTS All Supervisor, Diver, Diver/Tender, and Tender applicants are required to: Pass a hair follicle drug screen and background check Possess both, a government issued document to establish identity, and employment authorization Have a graduation certificate or card from either an ADCE accredited Commercial Diving Program or The United States Navy Hardhat Diving Program or hold an appropriate ADCI certification card Possess a First Aid/CPR/AED & Oxygen Provider Cert. Have a fit to dive physical Having a TWIC Card is helpful and required in certain circumstances Pay is based on experience & reliability All employees are subject to random drug screenings per USCG requirements How to Apply: e-mail your resume to resumes@aidc.us.com You will receive an automated response confirming our receipt of your resume Available to take your call at anytime!
