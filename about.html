About Us

American Inshore Divers Corp. is an underwater service business with its headquarters located in South Houston, Texas, providing skilled services for the construction, shipping, marine, oil, and gas industries throughout the Continental United States and Caribbean. Specializing in every aspect of underwater construction, maintenence, and repair.

Proud Members of:

  • The Association of Diving Contractors International
  • The U.S. Gulf of Mexico Diving Safety Work Group
  • Texas City "Reciprocal" Safety Council

OUR CORE OBJECTIVES

EXPERIENCE - Having the knowledge and skills for problem solving in a cost efficient and timely manner INTEGRITY - Adherence to strong, moral, and ethical principles and values SAFETY - Committed to the highest level of safety for each phase of your project

LOSS PREVENTION AND INDEMNITY

All company diving activities meet or exceed:

  • OSHA Instruction: CFR 29, part 1910, sub-part T, Commercial Diving Operations
  • HOMELAND SECURITY USCG CFR 46, Chapter 1, Subchapter V, part 197, sub-part B- Commercial Diving Operations
  • The Association of Diving Contractors International Consensus Standards for Commercial Diving and Underwater Operations 6.2 Edition
  • The USACE EM385-1-1, Section 30, Contract Diving Operations
  • American Inshore Divers Corp.'s Surface Diving Manual and HS&E Manual
